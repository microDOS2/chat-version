Visual Microdosing Guide

Each tablet is scored for precise dosing. Use this guide to tailor your experience.

1 Tablet

≈ 500 mg shrooms

Creative or recreational dose. Expect pronounced effects within ~15 minutes, ~4h window.

1/2 Tablet

≈ 250 mg shrooms

Standard microdose: noticeable lift in mood and creativity with minimal disruption.

1/4 Tablet

≈ 125 mg shrooms

Light microdose: subtle, sub‑perceptual focus and energy.

Safety & Use

  • Start low: New users should begin with 1/4 tab to assess sensitivity.
  • Legal: 4‑HO‑MET is not federally scheduled in the U.S.; verify your local laws.
  • Set & setting: Even with microdoses, be intentional about environment.
